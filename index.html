Welcome to Lucky Shrub.

Discover the beauty of nature with Lucky Shrub, your premier destination for garden design, landscaping, and a diverse selection of indoor and outdoor plants in Tucson, Arizona. Founded by Jason and Maria, our passion for plants and commitment to quality craftsmanship drive everything we do.

At Lucky Shrub, we specialize in transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary gardens. Whether you're dreaming of a serene backyard retreat or a vibrant urban oasis, our team is here to bring your vision to life. From initial concept to meticulous installation and ongoing maintenance, we provide comprehensive services tailored to meet your unique needs.

Explore our plant nursery, where you'll find a curated collection of plants suited for every environment and aesthetic. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our experts are here to assist you in selecting the perfect plants for your home or business.

Contact us at Lucky Shrub, we are always delighted to hear from you and answer any questions you may have. Whether you need advice on garden design, assistance with maintenance and landscaping, or want to learn more about our plants and nursery, our team is here to help.
